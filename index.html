RentSmart

RentSmart is a full-stack web application demo showing (hypothetical) reviews for landlords in the Denver area. A partner and I created it together while in our General Assembly web development immersive using Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and Bootstrap.

I had the idea for this application came after dealing with a rough landlord situation in another city. I wanted to give tenants the ability to give feedback on their own renting experience.
